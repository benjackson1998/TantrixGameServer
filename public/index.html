🎮 How to play this app

👆 Select a tile from your hand panel, then tap a hex on the board to place it.
↻ Rotate your selected tile using the ↻ Rotate button at the bottom before placing.

❓ How to play

Placing a tile

👆Tap a tile in your hand panel to select it. It will highlight green.
🎯Tap a hex on the board to place it. Colours must match all neighbours.

Rotating a tile

↻With a tile selected, press ↻ Rotate in the bottom bar to spin it 60°. Tap multiple times to cycle through all 6 orientations.
⌨️On desktop, press R on your keyboard to rotate.

Navigating the board

👆Drag (one finger / click-drag) to pan around the board.
🤏Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out on mobile.

Forced & blocked spaces

🟠Amber hexes are forced spaces — you must fill one before making a free move.
✕Dark hexes with ✕ are controlled sides — blocked until the adjacent forced space is filled.

Online multiplayer

🌐Coming soon! Online multiplayer is currently in development.

Scoring

📏Longest connected line of your colour = 1pt per tile.
⟳A closed loop scores double — 2pts per tile in the loop.
